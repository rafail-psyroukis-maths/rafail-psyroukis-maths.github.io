Rafail Psyroukis




Email Address: rafail.psyroukis [at] durham.ac.uk



My name is Rafail Psyroukis and I am a final year PhD student at Durham University in the UK. My supervisor is Prof. Thanasis Bouganis.

Previously, I completed my Bachelor's and Master's degree (Part III) from the University of Cambridge.

I am mainly interested in the study of higher rank modular forms. More specifically, my research interests include:

  • Siegel, Hermitian and Orthogonal Modular Forms.
  • Fourier-Jacobi Forms, Dirichlet Series and Differential Operators.
  • The spinor L-function (for Siegel and Hermitian modular forms).
  • The standard L-function (for orthogonal modular forms).
